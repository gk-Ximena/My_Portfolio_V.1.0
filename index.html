Sobre mí

Two threads, one weave.

I'm a web designer and full-stack developer finishing my Diploma in Computer Studies at Langara College (Dean's Honour Roll), building toward graduation in August 2026. My work sits at the intersection of two crafts: writing clean, accessible React and JavaScript, and designing interfaces people actually enjoy using.

I grew up around Peruvian textiles & pattern — the stepped crosses, the grid-woven color-blocking of Andean aguayo cloth — and I see the same logic in pixel art and in CSS grids: small, disciplined units building something expressive. That instinct shows up in everything I make, from a Rust-powered desktop widget to a bilingual educational platform.
